# PhotonRail: Optical Fabric Scheduling for AI Datacenters

NSDI 2026 technical report draft.

This document studies optical interconnect and datacenter networking for AI cluster fabrics. It evaluates wavelength scheduling, optical circuit switching, and congestion-aware routing with a prototype testbed and packet traces.

The evaluation compares against an electrical Clos baseline and reports p95 and p99 latency, throughput, and recovery behavior under link loss. It discusses silicon photonics, SerDes, BER, FEC, switch reconfiguration time, power, thermal stability, observability, rollback, and operations constraints.
